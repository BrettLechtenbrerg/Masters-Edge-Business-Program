==========================================
HOW TO BUILD YOUR BRAND BOOK
==========================================

This takes about 45-90 minutes and you'll have a
complete, professional brand book when you're done.

==========================================

STEP 1: GO TO CLAUDE
--------------------
Open your web browser and go to: claude.ai
Log in to your account (you need Claude Pro - $20/month)


STEP 2: CREATE A PROJECT
------------------------
1. Click "Projects" on the left side
2. Click "+ Create Project"
3. Name it "My Brand Book"
4. Click "Create"


STEP 3: ADD THE INSTRUCTIONS
----------------------------
1. Click the gear icon (settings) in your project
2. Find "Custom Instructions"
3. Open the file: "Build My Brand - Copy This Into Claude.txt"
4. Select ALL the text and COPY it
5. PASTE it into the Custom Instructions box
6. Click Save


STEP 4: START BUILDING
----------------------
1. Click "New Chat"
2. Type: Let's create my brand book
3. Press Enter
4. Answer the questions Claude asks you
5. Take your time - good answers = good brand book!


THAT'S IT!
----------
Claude will guide you through all 15 sections.
At the end, you'll have your complete brand book.


TIPS:
- If you get stuck, say "I'm not sure, give me options"
- You can take breaks - your chat saves automatically
- Want to change something? Say "Let's revise that"
- When done, say "Give me my complete brand book"
