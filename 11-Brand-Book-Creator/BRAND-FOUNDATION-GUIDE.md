# Brand Foundation Interview Guide
## Deep Discovery Questions for Your Brand's Core Identity

---

**Purpose:** This guide provides in-depth questions to uncover your brand foundation. Use it before the main Brand Book Creator for deeper self-reflection, or work through it with a partner/team.

**Time Required:** 60-90 minutes
**Best Done:** With a notebook, away from distractions

---

## Part 1: Brand Purpose Discovery

Your brand purpose answers: "Why do we exist beyond making money?"

### Reflection Questions

**The Origin Story:**
1. What problem in the world bothered you enough to start this business?
2. What did you see that others weren't addressing?
3. If money weren't an issue, would you still do this work? Why?

**The Impact Test:**
4. If your business disappeared tomorrow, what would the world lose?
5. What change do you want to see in your industry/community?
6. Finish this sentence: "The world would be better if more businesses..."

**The Legacy Question:**
7. In 20 years, what do you want people to say your business contributed?

### Purpose Statement Formula

```
"To [action verb] + [who you serve] + [the transformation you create]"

Examples:
- "To empower small business owners to reclaim their time and grow with confidence"
- "To prove that [belief] by [how you do it]"
- "To make [outcome] accessible to [who]"
```

## Part 2: Vision Statement Discovery

Your vision answers: "What does the future look like if we succeed?"

### Reflection Questions

**The Dream State:**
1. Close your eyes. It's 10 years from now and you've wildly succeeded. What does your industry look like?
2. How are your customers' lives different?
3. What's possible now that wasn't before?

**The Ripple Effect:**
4. If every business in your industry operated like you, how would things change?
5. What would your employees' lives look like?
6. What would your community look like?

**The Bold Claim:**
7. What seems impossible now but you believe you can make happen?

### Vision Statement Formula

```
"[A future state] where [what's different/better]"

Examples:
- "A future where AI makes work more human, not less"
- "A world where every small business has access to enterprise-level tools"
- "An industry where [negative thing] is obsolete"
```

## Part 3: Mission Statement Discovery

Your mission answers: "How do we achieve the vision day-to-day?"

### Reflection Questions

**The Daily Work:**
1. What do you actually DO for customers? (Be specific)
2. What's the experience like for someone working with you?
3. What do you do that competitors don't/won't?

**The Commitment:**
4. What promise do you make to every customer?
5. What will you ALWAYS do, no matter what?
6. What will you NEVER do, even if it costs you?

**The Method:**
7. If you had to describe your approach in 3 words, what would they be?

### Mission Statement Formula

```
"We [action] + [for whom] + [through what method] + [until what outcome]"

Examples:
- "We partner with businesses to embrace AI through consulting, implementation, and training—until every team member is confident"
- "We help [who] achieve [what] by [how], with [unique approach]"

## Part 4: Brand Positioning Discovery

Your positioning answers: "Why should someone choose you over alternatives?"

### Reflection Questions

**The Competitive Landscape:**
1. Who are your top 3 competitors? What are they known for?
2. What do most competitors in your space do that frustrates customers?
3. What's the "standard" way things are done that you disagree with?

**Your Differentiation:**
4. What do you do that NO ONE else does?
5. What do you do BETTER than anyone else?
6. What would a customer say about you that they wouldn't say about competitors?

**The Trade-Off:**
7. What do you deliberately NOT do that competitors do?
8. Who is NOT a good fit for you?

### Positioning Statement Formula

```
"Unlike [competitor approach/category], [Your Brand] [does what differently], ensuring [unique outcome] for [specific audience]."

Examples:
- "Unlike AI consultants who focus solely on efficiency and automation, Total Success AI puts people at the center—ensuring technology empowers individuals, strengthens teams, and creates lasting success."

## Part 5: Tagline Discovery

Your tagline is the memorable phrase that captures your essence.

### Tagline Types

**The Promise:** What you deliver
- "Just Do It" (Nike)
- "Think Different" (Apple)

**The Method:** How you do it
- "The Ultimate Driving Machine" (BMW)

**The Transformation:** What becomes possible
- "Open Happiness" (Coca-Cola)

**The Belief:** What you stand for
- "Because You're Worth It" (L'Oreal)

### Tagline Workshop

Answer these, then look for patterns:

1. What do customers feel AFTER working with you?
2. In 3-5 words, what's the transformation you create?
3. What belief drives everything you do?
4. What would you put on a t-shirt?
5. Finish this: "[Your Brand]: Where ___________"

### Tagline Rules
- 3-8 words ideal
- Avoid jargon
- Should be memorable and repeatable
- Bonus: Works both with and without your name

## Validation Checklist

Before finalizing, test your foundation:

- [ ] Could a new employee explain your purpose?
- [ ] Does your vision excite you when you read it?
- [ ] Does your mission accurately describe what you do?
- [ ] Would customers recognize themselves in your positioning?
- [ ] Is your tagline memorable after hearing it once?
- [ ] Does everything feel authentically YOU?
- [ ] Would you be proud to put this on your website?
