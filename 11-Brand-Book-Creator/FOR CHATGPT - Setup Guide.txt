HOW TO BUILD YOUR BRAND BOOK
USING CHATGPT
============================================

This guide is for ChatGPT users.
(If you use Claude, see the "FOR CLAUDE" files instead)

You need: ChatGPT Plus subscription ($20/month)

============================================

OPTION A: CREATE YOUR OWN CUSTOM GPT
(Takes 5 minutes to set up, then use forever)

--------------------------------------------

STEP 1: GO TO CHATGPT
Open your browser and go to: chat.openai.com
Log in to your ChatGPT Plus account


STEP 2: CREATE A NEW GPT
1. Click your profile picture (bottom left)
2. Click "My GPTs"
3. Click "+ Create a GPT"


STEP 3: CONFIGURE YOUR GPT
Click the "Configure" tab at the top, then fill in:

NAME:
Brand Book Creator

DESCRIPTION:
Create a complete, professional brand book for your business

INSTRUCTIONS:
Open the file "FOR CHATGPT - Brand Book Instructions.txt"
Copy ONLY the text between START and STOP
Paste it here


STEP 4: SAVE YOUR GPT
1. Click "Save" in the top right
2. Choose "Only me" (or share with others if you want)
3. Click "Confirm"


STEP 5: USE IT!
1. Your GPT now appears in "My GPTs"
2. Click it to start a new chat
3. Type: "Let's create my brand book"
4. Follow along as it guides you through 15 sections


============================================

OPTION B: JUST PASTE INTO A REGULAR CHAT
(Quicker but doesn't save for next time)

--------------------------------------------

1. Go to chat.openai.com
2. Start a new chat
3. Open "FOR CHATGPT - Brand Book Instructions.txt"
4. Copy the text between START and STOP
5. Paste it into the chat
6. Press Enter
7. Then type: "Let's create my brand book"


============================================

TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

- Take your time with answers
- If stuck, say "I'm not sure, give me options"
- You can take breaks and come back
- Say "let's revise that" to change anything
- At the end, say "give me my complete brand book"
