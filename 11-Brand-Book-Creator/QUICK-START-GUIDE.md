# Brand Book Creator - Quick Start Guide
## Create Your Brand Book in 3 Simple Steps

## What You'll Get

A complete, professional **15-section brand book** including:
- Your purpose, vision, and mission
- Brand voice and personality
- Target audience profiles
- Visual identity guidelines
- Key messages and elevator pitches
- And much more...

**Time:** 45-90 minutes | **Cost:** Free with Claude Pro ($20/mo)

## Step 1: Set Up Your Claude Project (5 minutes)

1. **Go to** [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and log in
2. **Click** "Projects" in the left sidebar
3. **Click** "+ Create Project"
4. **Name it** "My Brand Book"
5. **Click** the gear icon (‚öôÔ∏è) to open Project Settings
6. **Find** "Custom Instructions" section
7. **Open** the file `CLAUDE-PROJECT-INSTRUCTIONS.md`
8. **Copy EVERYTHING** from that file
9. **Paste** into the Custom Instructions box
10. **Save**

---

## Step 2: Start Your Brand Book (2 minutes)

1. **Click** "New Chat" in your project
2. **Type:** "Let's create my brand book"
3. **Hit Enter**

That's it! Claude will guide you from here.

---

## Step 3: Follow Along (45-90 minutes)

Claude will walk you through each section:

| Section | What Claude Will Ask |
|---------|---------------------|
| Foundation | Why you exist, your vision, values |
| Story | How you started, challenges overcome |
| Voice | How your brand "sounds" |
| Audience | Who you serve |
| Messages | What you want people to remember |
| Visuals | Colors, fonts, imagery |
| ...and more | 15 sections total |
