# Visual Identity Worksheet
## Defining Your Brand's Visual Language

---

**Purpose:** This worksheet helps you document and define the visual elements of your brand—colors, typography, logo usage, and imagery style. Even if you work with a designer, this gives you the vocabulary and framework to communicate your vision.

**Time Required:** 30-45 minutes
**Prerequisite:** You should have your Brand Foundation (Section 1) and Brand Personality (Section 6) completed first.

---

## Part 1: Color Psychology Alignment

Before choosing colors, understand what they communicate:

| Color | Psychology | Best For |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Blue** | Trust, stability, professionalism | Finance, tech, healthcare |
| **Navy** | Authority, sophistication, depth | Consulting, luxury, B2B |
| **Green** | Growth, health, nature, wealth | Wellness, finance, eco |
| **Red** | Energy, urgency, passion | Food, entertainment, sales |
| **Orange** | Creativity, enthusiasm, warmth | Youth, creative, food |
| **Yellow** | Optimism, clarity, warmth | Children, energy, budget |
| **Purple** | Luxury, creativity, wisdom | Beauty, coaching, premium |
| **Pink** | Care, softness, femininity | Beauty, children, wellness |
| **Black** | Sophistication, luxury, power | Fashion, luxury, tech |
| **White** | Cleanliness, simplicity, modern | Healthcare, tech, minimal |
| **Gray** | Neutrality, balance, professionalism | Tech, industrial, modern |

## Part 3: Typography Selection

### Font Psychology

| Font Type | Communicates | Best For |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Serif** (Times, Georgia) | Traditional, trustworthy, established | Law, finance, publishing |
| **Sans-Serif** (Inter, Helvetica) | Modern, clean, approachable | Tech, startups, minimal |
| **Slab Serif** (Rockwell, Roboto Slab) | Bold, confident, impactful | Marketing, headlines |
| **Script** (Pacifico, Dancing Script) | Elegant, personal, creative | Luxury, wedding, beauty |
| **Display** (Bebas, Playfair) | Distinctive, attention-grabbing | Headlines, logos |
| **Monospace** (Courier, Source Code) | Technical, precise | Tech, coding, documentation |
