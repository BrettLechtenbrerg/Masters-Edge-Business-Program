# Conversation Preparation Framework
## Difficult Conversations Coach - Deep Preparation Worksheet

---

## Overview

**Purpose:** A comprehensive worksheet for thoroughly preparing for difficult conversations. Use this when the stakes are high and you want to be fully ready.

**Time Required:** 30-45 minutes

**When to Use:**
- Before high-stakes conversations (terminations, major performance issues)
- When you've had failed conversations before with this person
- When you're emotionally charged and need to process first
- When the relationship is particularly important to preserve

### Part 9: Pre-Conversation Checklist

```
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                    FINAL CHECKLIST
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

BEFORE THE CONVERSATION:

Mindset
□ I've considered their perspective genuinely
□ I've identified my part in this
□ I'm going in curious, not certain
□ I'm prepared to learn something new
□ I'm emotionally regulated (not reactive)

Practical
□ Right time scheduled (not rushed, not Friday afternoon)
□ Right place (private, comfortable)
□ Enough time blocked
□ I won't be interrupted
□ I've practiced my opening

Materials (if applicable)
□ Documentation I need
□ Notes for myself (not to read from, just to glance at)
□ Any resources to share


DURING THE CONVERSATION, I WILL:

□ Lead with curiosity
□ Listen more than I talk
□ Acknowledge their perspective
□ Own my part
□ Stay focused on understanding, not winning
□ Watch for my own defensiveness
□ Give them time to process
□ Confirm understanding before moving forward


AFTER THE CONVERSATION, I WILL:

□ Follow up on any commitments I made
□ Send any agreed-upon documentation
□ Check in at an appropriate time
□ Reflect on what I learned
□ Adjust my approach based on what happened

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
