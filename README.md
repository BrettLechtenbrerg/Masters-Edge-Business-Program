## Welcome

This folder contains the complete Master's Edge Business Program - a collection of AI-powered prompts, tools, and systems designed to help business owners and managers:

- **Automate** repetitive tasks and decisions
- **Elevate** their role from operator to strategist
- **Focus** on the people-first elements of their business
- **Scale** their business without sacrificing their life

## Flagship System #1: The Delegation Engine

The **Delegation Engine** is a comprehensive 5-component system that helps business owners systematically identify what they should stop doing, calculate the true cost of low-value work, and create actionable plans to free their time.

| Component | Purpose | Time |
|-----------|---------|------|
| [Main Engine](./07-Delegation-Engine/DELEGATION-ENGINE.md) | Complete delegation prioritization system | 60-90 min |
| [Time Audit](./07-Delegation-Engine/TIME-AUDIT-WORKSHEET.md) | Track where your time actually goes | 5 min/day or 30 min estimate |
| [Value Calculator](./07-Delegation-Engine/TASK-VALUE-CALCULATOR.md) | Calculate the dollar value of every task | 20-30 min |
| [Delegation Plan](./07-Delegation-Engine/DELEGATION-PLAN-TEMPLATE.md) | Create detailed handoff plans | 15-30 min per task |
| [Job Description Generator](./07-Delegation-Engine/JOB-DESCRIPTION-GENERATOR.md) | Turn delegations into hiring posts | 20-30 min per role |

**Why This System is Unique:** Nothing like this exists anywhere else. Every other tool focuses on task management, not helping owners answer *"What am I doing that I shouldn't be doing?"*

## Flagship System #2: The SOP Factory

The **SOP Factory** extracts the processes trapped in your head and turns them into clear, repeatable Standard Operating Procedures - complete with training plans and GHL automation blueprints.

| Component | Purpose | Time |
|-----------|---------|------|
| [Main Factory](./08-SOP-Factory/SOP-FACTORY.md) | Conversational process extraction | 20-60 min |
| [SOP Template](./08-SOP-Factory/SOP-TEMPLATE.md) | Ready-to-use document formats | Reference |
| [Process Interview](./08-SOP-Factory/PROCESS-INTERVIEW-GUIDE.md) | Deep-dive question bank | 30-60 min |
| [Training Generator](./08-SOP-Factory/TRAINING-PLAN-GENERATOR.md) | Turn SOPs into training programs | 20-30 min |
| [GHL Automation](./08-SOP-Factory/GHL-AUTOMATION-BLUEPRINT.md) | Convert SOPs to GHL workflows | 15-30 min |

**Why This System is Unique:** Most SOPs are useless documents that collect dust. This system creates SOPs through conversation, then immediately converts them into training programs AND Go High Level automations.

## Flagship System #3: The Difficult Conversations Coach

The **Difficult Conversations Coach** transforms dreaded workplace conversations into growth opportunities through psychological safety principles, structured preparation, and AI-powered practice sessions. Built to be congruent with the Performance Review Pro software.

| Component | Purpose | Time |
|-----------|---------|------|
| [Main Coach](./09-Difficult-Conversations-Coach/DIFFICULT-CONVERSATIONS-COACH.md) | Prepare & practice difficult conversations | 30-45 min |
| [Scenario Library](./09-Difficult-Conversations-Coach/SCENARIO-LIBRARY.md) | 10 pre-built roleplay scenarios | 15-30 min each |
| [Conversation Prep](./09-Difficult-Conversations-Coach/CONVERSATION-PREP.md) | Deep preparation worksheet | 30-45 min |
| [De-escalation Toolkit](./09-Difficult-Conversations-Coach/DE-ESCALATION-TOOLKIT.md) | Techniques for when things get heated | Reference |

**Why This System is Unique:** Most "difficult conversation" training focuses on delivering bad news. This system focuses on understanding, psychological safety, and growth - transforming tough talks into relationship-strengthening moments.

**Congruent With:** Performance Review Pro software (same psychological safety principles, growth-focused language, bidirectional understanding)

## Flagship System #4: The Hiring Oracle

The **Hiring Oracle** transforms your hiring process from gut-feel guessing to systematic culture-fit assessment. Find people who will genuinely thrive in your environment - not just fill a seat.

| Component | Purpose | Time |
|-----------|---------|------|
| [Main Oracle](./10-Hiring-Oracle/HIRING-ORACLE.md) | Culture-first interview preparation system | 30-45 min |
| [Interview Questions](./10-Hiring-Oracle/INTERVIEW-QUESTIONS.md) | 150+ proven questions by category | Reference |
| [Candidate Scorecard](./10-Hiring-Oracle/CANDIDATE-SCORECARD.md) | Structured evaluation framework | 15-20 min per candidate |
| [Reference Check Guide](./10-Hiring-Oracle/REFERENCE-CHECK-GUIDE.md) | Get real information from references | 15-20 min per reference |
| [Hiring Decision Framework](./10-Hiring-Oracle/HIRING-DECISION-FRAMEWORK.md) | When to hire, pass, or explore more | 20-30 min |

**Why This System is Unique:** Most hiring focuses on skills and experience. This system focuses on the four dimensions of fit: Values, Culture, Role, and Growth. Because values can't be trained, but skills can.

**Connects With:** Job Description Generator (creates the role), Delegation Plan (onboards the hire), Performance Review Pro (ongoing evaluation)

## Recommended Learning Path

### Week 1: Foundation
- **Fire Yourself Exercise** - Understand your current role reality
- **Ideal Week Designer** - Design your target schedule

### Week 2: Decisions & Communication
- **Decision Journal** - Start documenting major decisions
- **Problem Explainer** - Practice articulating one current challenge

### Week 3: External Relationships
- **Vendor Negotiation Prep** - Prep for your next vendor renewal
- **Partnership Evaluator** - Evaluate any current or pending partnerships

### Week 4: Delegation Mastery
- **Delegation Engine** - Complete the full assessment
- Create delegation plans for top 3 tasks
- Generate job descriptions if hiring needed

### Week 5: Systems & Automation
- **SOP Factory** - Document your most critical process
- Create training plans for each SOP
- Build GHL automations from your SOPs

### Week 6: People Leadership
- **Difficult Conversations Coach** - Prepare for any pending tough conversations
- Practice using the Scenario Library
- Review De-escalation techniques

### Week 7: Hiring Excellence
- **Hiring Oracle** - Prepare for your next interview
- Build your culture-fit question set
- Practice the four-dimension evaluation

### Ongoing
- Use Decision Journal for every major decision
- Weekly Delegation Engine check-ins (15 min)
- Document new SOPs as processes emerge
- Use Conversations Coach before any difficult talk
- Use Hiring Oracle for every hire
- Revisit Fire Yourself quarterly
- Update Ideal Week monthly

## Philosophy: The Master's Edge

**The Master's Edge** is about achieving mastery in business through:

1. **Clarity** - Know what matters, ignore what doesn't
2. **Systems** - Build once, benefit forever
3. **Leverage** - Do less, achieve more
4. **People** - Technology enables human connection, not replaces it

These tools are designed to give business owners back their most precious resource: **time to focus on what only humans can do** - building relationships, leading teams, and creating value.
