# Candidate Evaluation Scorecard
## The Hiring Oracle - Systematic Candidate Assessment

---

## Overview

**Purpose:** Provide a structured, consistent way to evaluate candidates across all four dimensions of fit - reducing bias and ensuring you're comparing candidates fairly.

**When to Use:** Complete immediately after each interview while impressions are fresh.

**Why It Matters:** Structured evaluation reduces bias, improves hiring decisions, and creates documentation you can reference when making final decisions.

### Score Interpretation

| Average Score | Interpretation |
|---------------|----------------|
| 4.5 - 5.0 | **Strong Hire** - Move quickly, they'll have options |
| 3.5 - 4.4 | **Likely Hire** - Proceed with references and final evaluation |
| 2.5 - 3.4 | **Needs More Info** - Second interview or deeper reference check |
| 1.5 - 2.4 | **Likely Pass** - Concerns outweigh strengths |
| 1.0 - 1.4 | **Clear Pass** - Don't proceed |

**Important:** A single score of 1 in any VALUES dimension should be seriously considered as a dealbreaker, regardless of other scores.
